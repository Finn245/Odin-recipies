Lasagna

description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

ingredients

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Beef
  • Tomato Paste
  • Parmsan
  • Salt
  • Onion

steps

  1. Use a store-bought pasta sauce that tastes great.
  2. Make sure the lasagna noodles are completely covered in the sauce.
  3. Let it rest on a cooling rack for at least 15 minutes before slicing a square.
